Born in Nepal. Guided by the Himalayas.

Kora is a modern sanctuary inspired by Himalayan principles of balance and inner strength. We bring movement, recovery, and community together so you can grow in mind, body, and spirit—at a grounded, sustainable pace.

Our name comes from the Tibetan practice of circumambulation—a mindful journey taken with purpose.
